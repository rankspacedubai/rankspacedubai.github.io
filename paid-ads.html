Common Questions

FAQ

What is the minimum ad budget I need?
We recommend a minimum ad spend of AED 1,500/month to see meaningful results. This is separate from our management fee and goes directly to Google or Meta. For competitive industries like real estate or medical, AED 3,000+ gives better results.
How soon will I see leads from ads?
Google Search Ads can drive leads within the first few days. Meta ads typically take 1–2 weeks to exit the learning phase and optimize. We usually see the best performance from week 3 onwards as the algorithm learns your audience.
Do you create the ad graphics and copy?
Yes — our management packages include ad copywriting and basic creative design. For video ads or high-production creatives, that's available as an add-on or within the Dominate package.
Can I pause or stop the campaigns anytime?
Yes. You own your ad accounts and can pause campaigns at any time. We recommend a minimum 3-month commitment to see real optimization results, but there are no long-term lock-in contracts.
How do you measure success?
We track leads, calls, form fills, sales, and cost per conversion — not just clicks and impressions. Every monthly report shows exactly what your budget generated in terms of real business results.
