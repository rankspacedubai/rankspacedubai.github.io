Common Questions

FAQ

How long does it take to build a website?
Most websites are delivered within 7–14 business days depending on the complexity and how quickly you provide content (text, images, logo).
Do I need to buy hosting and domain separately?
Yes — domain and hosting are purchased separately. We guide you through the process and can even set it up on your behalf. Typical costs are AED 50–200/year for a domain and AED 200–600/year for hosting.
Can I update the website myself after it's built?
Yes! WordPress and Shopify sites are built so you can easily update text, images, and content yourself. We also provide a short handover tutorial.
Is SEO included in the website package?
Basic on-page SEO (meta titles, descriptions, image alt tags, sitemap) is included in all packages. Advanced SEO is available as an add-on service.
What if I already have a website and want a redesign?
Absolutely, we do redesigns too. Share your current site with us and we'll assess what needs improving and provide a custom quote.
